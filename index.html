What we do

We're the bookkeeping team for businesses that have outgrown their accountant — but aren't ready for a full-time CFO.

Frankly is a small, deliberate team of CPAs and ex-finance folks who do one thing properly: keep your books clean, your reports honest, and your tax surprises to a minimum.

We work with growing Canadian and US businesses — construction crews, design studios, therapy practices, e-comm brands, agencies, restaurants — anyone who'd rather be running their business than reconciling it.

No app. No platform. No "AI co-pilot dashboard."
Just people who pick up the phone.
